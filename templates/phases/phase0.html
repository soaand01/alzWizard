Phase 0 â€” Planning

Prepare the architecture and decisions that drive your Landing Zone deployment.

Purpose: Capture design decisions (IaC choice, management groups, subscription strategy, naming, and networking) so bootstrap and deployment steps are repeatable and auditable.

Before you start
  • Stakeholders: Identify project owner and a tenant administrator who can create app registrations and assign roles.
  • Documentation: Have the official ALZ docs open and the planning checklist downloaded.
Steps
  1. Download the planning checklist: checklist.xlsx and open it in your preferred spreadsheet editor.
  2. Choose IaC tooling (Bicep or Terraform) and complete the corresponding tabs in the checklist.
  3. Define management group layout and create a list of subscription names and intended regions.
  4. Record starter module and configuration choices for your scenario (see Scenarios).
  5. Save the completed checklist for later phases; it will be used to populate bootstrap configuration.
Verify
  • Checklist saved with management group and subscription mappings.
  • Starter module and IaC choice recorded.
